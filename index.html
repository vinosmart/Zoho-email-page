Hi Eric,

There are things I love and hate about Aussie food.

Being American, I loathe Australian hamburgers and lack of quality Mexican food. I love the amount of Asian restaurants available in the cities, and you can take me out for a pub dinner (schnitzel) any day of the week. My fascination with Australia started young and was probably enhanced as a tween when I fell in love with Silver chair. I always thought it sounded like an exotic place to travel, and you never meet an Assie traveling that doesn't love their home country.

Thank you..
